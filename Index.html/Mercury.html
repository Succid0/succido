Mercury

The Swiftest Planet

Quick Facts About Mercury

  • Closest Planet: Mercury is the closest planet to the Sun.
  • Smallest Planet: It is also the smallest planet in the Solar System.
  • No Atmosphere: Mercury has a very thin atmosphere, which means it can’t trap heat.
  • Extreme Temperatures: Surface temperatures can reach up to 430°C during the day and drop to -180°C at night.
  • Cratered Surface: Its surface is covered in craters from meteoroid impacts.
  • Short Year: A year on Mercury lasts just 88 Earth days.
  • Long Days: A single day on Mercury lasts 176 Earth days.
