Earth

Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only known place in the universe where life exists. It has a rich and diverse environment, including oceans, continents, and an atmosphere that sustains life. Earth orbits the Sun every 365.25 days and has one natural satellite, the Moon. It has a protective magnetic field and atmosphere, shielding it from harmful solar radiation. Earth's surface is 71% water, making it unique among known planets.
