Europa

Europa is one of Jupiter’s largest moons and is slightly smaller than Earth’s Moon. Beneath its icy crust lies a vast ocean that may harbor conditions suitable for life. Its surface is smooth and bright, crisscrossed by long, linear fractures, indicating a relatively young and active surface. Europa is a primary target for future missions searching for extraterrestrial life in our Solar System.
